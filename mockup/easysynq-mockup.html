This procedure defines how the organization selects, evaluates and re-evaluates external providers of materials and outsourced processes that affect product conformity, in accordance with ISO 9001:2015 Cl 8.4.

The Process Owner (Diego) maintains the approved-supplier list; Quality (Mara) authorizes re-evaluation thresholds; Purchasing raises supplier change requests through the DCR workflow.
